Slime-San

Released on Steam and Nintendo Switch, this was my first commissioned job for an indie game developer. This soundtrack contains an all-star cast of chiptune musicians to make the game an awesome experience.

Game Description:

Slime-san was minding his own business, sliming around in a peaceful forest when suddenly ... A giant worm appeared and gobbled him up! Now deep within the worm's belly, Slime-san has to face a decision: Be digested by the incoming wall of stomach acid... Or jump, slide and slime his way through the worms intestines and back out its mouth!

Super Slime Arena


About:

Released on Steam, I was asked to do a remix of my Slime-San track for a special stage included in Super Slime Arena.

Game Description:

Super Slime Arena is a 16-bit style, party-fighting game embracing equal parts accessibility and chaos. One attack, one hitpoint, all slime! Use nearly any controller in fast and frantic 2-50+ multiplayer matches across the many lands of Planet Blorb!

Cosmic Phantasm


About:

This one of my only "serious" releases on bandcamp. This album is a collection of Toho remixes from 2011-2014, all done on LSDJ for the Nintendo Gameboy.


About:
