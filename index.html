Bio:

My name's Christophe Goulet-LeBlanc aka Kommisar. I'm an Acadian chip music composer from Moncton NB, Composing music almost solely on a GameBoy tracker, LSDJ. The sound design is something more than just nostalgia. As chip musicians we aim to make full use of the limited channels that the Nintendo GameBoy has to offer. I'm currently a computer science student at Dalhousie University in Halifax, Nova Scotia. I usually make music for myself, but have occasionally contributed music to compilations, CDs, and indie games. If interested, please don't hesitate to contact me for any projects you feel I would be a good fit for.
